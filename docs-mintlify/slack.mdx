---
title: "Slack"
---

1. In Tracer, go to Integrations → Slack
2. Click Add to Slack
3. In Slack, pick your workspace and click **Allow**

   <Frame>
     ![Give Slack Access](/images/give_slack_access.png)
   </Frame>
4. In Slack, open your alerts channel. 
5. Type /add and click **Add apps to this channel**
6. Search **Tracer** and click **Add**
7. In Tracer, open **Integrations** → **Slack**. Under **Channel Mappings** click **\+Add.** Select the channels you’ve added Tracer to, and select which types of alerts you’d like Tracer to respond to.

8. If you don't see your channel here, you'll need to invite @Tracey to the channel in Slack. Go to your chosen channel and use the command `/invite @Tracey` to add it.

   <Frame>
     ![Channel Mapping Slack](/images/channel_mapping_slack.png)
   </Frame>
